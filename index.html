Battle Royale | First-Person Shooter

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale video game re-leased on March 10, 2020, for the Xbox one, Playstation 4, and PC the game is a part of the 2019 title Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, but does not require purchase of it. Warzone was developed by infinity ward and Raven Software and published by Activision. Warzone allows online multi-player combat among 150 players set in the fictional city of cerdansk and features both cross-platform play and cross-platform progression between both games.
